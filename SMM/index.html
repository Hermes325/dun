Social Media Marketing

Social Media Optimization (SMO) is one of the two pillars of Social Media Marketing. This side is about catching your target audience and getting free traffic to your website from social media networks such as Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, Google+, and others through social activities like blogging, social bookmarking, storytelling, user rating, feedback, and much more.

Instagram
